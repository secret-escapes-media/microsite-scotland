The Stay

You and a special someone will spend three nights at the boutique bolthole, The Grandtully Hotel by Ballintaggart. This gorgeous B&B set in the charming village of Grandtully promises chic elegance, with beautifully-designed rooms adding a modern touch to your stay. Don’t miss the chance to dine in the hotel’s elegant restaurant, or take the short drive to Ballintaggart Farm and indulge in the tasting menu where produce sourced from the kitchen garden and local artisans and suppliers makes for an irresistible feast.

The Experience

Enjoy a mini masterclass at The Cook School by Ballintaggart, where skilled chefs are ready to show you their insider tips for delicious dining; this will, naturally, be followed with a light lunch to settle your foodie cravings. Next, you’ll head out on your private Highland safari. With a knowledgeable ranger at your side, you’ll take to the private trails of this mountainous, wild landscape in a Land Rover. Spot red deer and golden eagles, if you’re lucky enough, with your ranger there to help you get the best out of this beautiful region.
